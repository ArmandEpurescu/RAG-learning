# Path of Exile Helper Ideas

The assistant can become useful for Path of Exile by indexing local notes and trusted build resources.

Useful context to collect:

1. Preferred playstyle: melee, ranged, minions, spells, traps, totems, or tanky builds.
2. League goals: campaign, mapping, bosses, currency farming, SSF, trade league, hardcore.
3. Budget ranges: league starter, low budget, medium budget, expensive endgame.
4. Build notes: skill gems, passive tree links, gear priorities, defenses, flasks, and leveling steps.
5. Common questions: what to upgrade next, why damage feels low, why survivability feels bad.

Potential tools:

1. Read local build notes from `data`.
2. Summarize a Path of Building export pasted into a text file.
3. Compare gear priorities from indexed notes.
4. Generate a checklist for campaign or early mapping.

The assistant should not claim current league meta knowledge unless fresh notes or sources were indexed.
